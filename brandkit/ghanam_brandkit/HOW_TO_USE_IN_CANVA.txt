Canva note:
- Cairo and Changa usually exist as built-in fonts in Canva. Search the font dropdown for: Cairo / Changa.
- Uploading custom fonts in Canva requires Canva Pro. If you're on Free, use built-in Cairo/Changa (or Tajawal as fallback).

How to add logo to your design:
1) Open the design link in Canva.
2) Left panel > Uploads > Upload files > select the logo file.
3) Drag the logo onto the canvas.
4) Place it top-right (Arabic) or top-left, keep clear space around it.
5) Export as PNG.
